Veg Thukpa

Description

Veg Thukpa is a warm and comforting noodle soup that originated in the Himalayan region. It is made with noodles, fresh vegetables, and flavorful seasonings cooked in a savory broth. Veg Thukpa is a popular dish because it is nutritious, easy to prepare, and perfect for cold weather.

Ingredients

  • 200 g noodles
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (thinly sliced)
  • 1 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 1 capsicum (sliced)
  • 4 cups vegetable stock or water
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the base by heating oil in a large pot. Add garlic, ginger, and onions, and sauté for 2–3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Cook the vegetables by adding carrots, cabbage, and capsicum. Stir-fry for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add the broth by pouring in the vegetable stock or water. Bring it to a boil.
  5. Season the soup with soy sauce, black pepper, and salt. Mix well and let it simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  6. Add the noodles to the soup and cook for another 2 minutes so the flavors combine.
  7. Serve hot in bowls and garnish with chopped spring onions before serving.
