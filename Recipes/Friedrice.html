Fried rice

Description

Egg Fried Rice is a quick and flavorful dish made with cooked rice, eggs, fresh vegetables, and simple seasonings. It is a popular meal that is easy to prepare and perfect for lunch or dinner. Using chilled rice helps create the best texture, while soy sauce and spices add a rich, savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and cooled rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, or beans)
  • 1–2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp paprika (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped spring onions (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare rice
    cooked and cooled rice. Break up any large lumps before cooking.
  2. Scramble eggs
    Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan. Add the beaten eggs and scramble until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. Cook the vegetables
    Add the remaining oil to the pan. Sauté the garlic and onions until fragrant, then add the mixed vegetables and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add the rice
    Add the cooked rice to the pan and stir well until combined with the vegetables.
  5. Season
    Add soy sauce, black pepper, paprika, and salt. Mix thoroughly so the rice is evenly coated.
  6. Add the eggs
    Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and mix everything together.
  7. Finish
    Cook for another 1–2 minutes on high heat. Garnish with chopped spring onions if desired and serve hot.
